Your Voice on Tenancy Issues

Toronto is facing significant housing challenges, from affordability and tenant rights to maintenance standards and eviction protections. This space is designed to help you engage with important tenancy issues affecting our city.

Use the interactive tool below to share your experiences, voice your concerns, and contribute to meaningful policy discussions that can help shape a more equitable housing landscape in Toronto.

Key Tenancy Issues in Toronto

  • Rent control and affordability protections
  • Eviction prevention and tenant security
  • Building maintenance and safety standards
  • Accessible housing options
  • Secondary suite regulations
  • Rental housing supply
